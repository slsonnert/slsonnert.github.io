Use reusable sandwich bags if you bring your own lunch

Reusable sandwich bags are just as easy to use as plastic ziplock bags and they can be washed in the dishwasher for many uses.

Recycle and compost whatever you can from your school lunch

If you get school lunch and your school has recycling and/or compostable bins, sort your trash into the different bins.

Walk, bike, or take public transportation

Walking and biking are a better alternative to driving to school. If you do not want to walk or bike, you can also take public transportation instead of driving.
