AT HOME

Buy a compostable toothbrush

Plastic toothbrushes take a long time to break down. Using a compostable toothbrush will help reduce the amount of waste going to landfills.

Use reusable grocery bags

Reusable grocery bags help reduce the number of plastic bags that get thrown away and/or that land in the ocean.

Use bar soap or buy refills of liquid soap

Bar soap requires less energy to produce and package. If you like liquid soap better than bar soap, you can use refills of the liquid soap instead of buying a new container.
